OXYMETHOLONE
Aquatest Magnus Pharmaceuticals is an injectable preparation containing pure water-based testosterone (without esters). Testosterone microcrystals without an ether base are a very popular injectable form, the period of activity of the active substance of which is 2-3 days. It was developed in the 1940s and after that a testosterone suspension was an indispensable drug.

SIDE EFFECTS
Oxymetholone is a highly estrogenic steroid. Gynecomastia is often a concern during treatment, and may present itself quite early into a cycle (particularly when higher doses are used). At the same time both subcutaneous water retention and fat levels build. Androgenic side effects are still possible with this substance.
ADMINISTRATION
An effective oral daily dosage would fall in the range of 25-150 mg, taken in cycles lasting no more than 6-8 weeks to minimize hepatotoxicity.
