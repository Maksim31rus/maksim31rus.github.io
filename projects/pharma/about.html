A little brief about us

Magnus Pharmaceuticals PVT

Magnus Pharmaceuticals PVT is one of the largest privately held pharmaceutical companies in India. It is dedicated to delivering premium quality generic medicines to the developing countries and beyond. We are trusted by healthcare professionals and patients around the globe. Over the last 15 years, we have strengthened our leadership in India's pharmaceutical industry. Additionally, we have also strengthened our global focus by consolidating and deepening our presence in the key markets of Asia, Africa and the Middle East.


At the moment, we are distributing our products in over 35 other countries around the world. Magnus Pharmaceuticals have progressed into a global organization and have evolved to become one of the most successful pharmaceutical companies across emerging markets.
